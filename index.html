Under 5's Eat free!

Here at Ahab’s Fine Dining, we know just how important it is for families to be able to eat with other another and share in a delicious meal. This kind of bonding is priceless which is what under 5’s get to eat for free along with being able to enjoy reduced portion sized upon request. At Ahab’s, theres an experience for all ages to enjoy!

3 Course Meals for £25!

Oftentimes, the price of a filling and succulent piece of seafood can get expensive very quickly as complicated preparation methods and crushing prestige makes some dishes hard to come by at your typical restaurant. But where not typical here at Ahab’s, here you can get a 3-course meal for £25 complete with Starter, Main and Dessert accompanied by one our fine craft ales. Don’t break the bank, break your shirt buttons instead!

2 for 1 on Craft Beer!

While we do value our wonderful fishermen who source our award winning produce we are also very in-touch with local breweries. Ahab’s Fine Dining offers a variety of craft beers from pale ales, to stouts to lagers all at buy one get one free so you enjoy the beverages you love. If you have a taste for hops then there will certainly be something for you to enjoy with your seafood of choice!
