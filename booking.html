OFFICIAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Contact: Telephone lines are open between 9.00am – 8.00pm Tuesday to saturday. Please leave a message if we can’t answer immediately and we will return your call as soon as possible. Please note we are closed on Sunday – Monday.

Payment: As soon as your details are recieved by this form we will send you a confirmation email to ensure that we will be able to accommodate you on the given dates and times. All booking are paid for on the day in person.

Note: We welcome children of all ageswho will enjoy our style of food. We do not offer a children’s menu. Please see our website for sample menus. We will prepare your food at a more appropriate portion size upon request.

Cancellation: We kindly request that diners give us at least 48 hours notice if a cancellation or amendment is required (and should be notified by email including your booking reference). Only in the event of a late cancellation or a no-show will a charge be made. You will be liable to a full charge at £10pp.

We kindly ask that groups are kept to a maximum of 4 due to capacity limitations in our restaraunt. Thank you.
